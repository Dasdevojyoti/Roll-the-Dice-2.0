Roll The Dice!

Rules and Regulations

1. Player who scores 100 first wins the game.

2. If you roll the dice 1 the control of the game will go to the next player and you will loose all the current score.

3. For adding the current score to the total you need to click the hold button and the current score will be added to the total score and the control will move to the next player.
